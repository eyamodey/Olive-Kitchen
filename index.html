CHEF FAITH TOM

Starting to cook at the age of 12, Faith passionately learned the secret of the local Nigerian cuisine from her mum who, by travel and exposure, knew how to prepare most local delicacies of the major tribes of Nigeria. She graduated from Dorbeh Polythecnic in 2017 and went on to do the mandatory National Youth Services Corp programme. When returning to Abuja in 2018, Faith discovered the thriving business of food selling and shelved picking up a white collar job with meager pay for her own food vendor business under an umbrella. Today that umbrella has metamorphosed into a fully functional restaurant with employed staff and growing.

A great place to experience Nigerian food!
